‚úÖ Eligibility Criteria

  • You must be at least 18 years old (or 16-17 with parental consent).
  • You must weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kg).
  • You must be in good health and feel well on the day of donation.
  • Have not donated blood in the last 56 days.
  • Have no recent tattoos or piercings (within the last 3 months).
  • Have not traveled to malaria-risk areas in the past year.

üìñ Frequently Asked Questions

Am I Eligible to Donate Blood?

To donate, you must be in good health, at least 18 years old, and meet certain health and lifestyle requirements.

How Often Can I Donate Blood?

Whole blood donations can be made every 56 days. Platelet donations can be made every 7 days, up to 24 times per year.

What Should I Do Before and After Donating?

Drink plenty of water, eat a healthy meal before donating, and avoid strenuous activities afterward.
